The Story

‎

The film follows two estranged sisters whose reunion is cut short by the rise of flesh-possessing demons. Trapped in a high-rise apartment, they must fight for their survival against pure evil unleashed by the Book of the Dead.

‎

It’s bloody, it’s dark, and it’s emotionally twisted — classic Evil Dead but with a fresh new setting.
